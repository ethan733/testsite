Three-Year Common Sense Warranty

World Class Piano Gallery, LLC (“Seller”) warrants that the piano purchased (“Instrument”) is free from defects in materials and workmanship that would affect its normal use and performance for a period of three (3) years from the date of delivery.

Coverage

  • Structural integrity of the piano, including the frame, soundboard, bridges, and keybed.
  • Action parts, keys, and pedal mechanisms under normal use.
  • Any defect in workmanship or materials that prevents the piano from functioning as intended.
  • Labor and parts necessary to correct covered defects.

What This Warranty Does Not Cover

  • Normal tuning, regulation, and voicing adjustments (routine maintenance).
  • Normal wear and tear of strings, hammers, dampers, and finish.
  • Damage from improper use, neglect, accidents, or extreme conditions (humidity, dryness, heat, cold, or water).
  • Work performed by technicians not authorized by World Class Piano Gallery.

Buyer’s Responsibility

  • Maintain the piano in a climate-controlled environment (40–60% humidity, 65–75°F).
  • Have the piano tuned and serviced regularly by a qualified technician.
  • Promptly notify World Class Piano Gallery of any warranty issues.

How Service Works

If a defect covered by this warranty arises, World Class Piano Gallery will, at its discretion:

  1. Repair the piano at no charge, or
  2. Replace defective parts, or
  3. If repair is not practical, provide a reasonable alternative solution.

All warranty service will be performed by or arranged through World Class Piano Gallery.

Limitations

This warranty is non-transferable and applies only to the original purchaser. It represents the full extent of coverage; no other express or implied warranties (including merchantability or fitness for purpose) apply beyond the terms stated herein.

Peace of Mind

This is a “common sense” warranty: if something goes wrong with your piano that should not reasonably happen under normal use and care, we will make it right within the three-year period.
