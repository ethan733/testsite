Our Warranty

At World Class Piano Gallery, we stand behind every instrument we deliver. Your piano is not just a purchase—it is an investment in craftsmanship, artistry, and performance.

3-Year Limited Warranty

Every piano purchased through our gallery is protected by a comprehensive 3-year warranty. This warranty covers defects in materials and workmanship under normal use during the warranty period.

  • Full coverage of all manufacturer defects
  • Complimentary in-home service by certified technicians
  • Free tuning adjustments during the warranty period
  • Priority access to our service team

This warranty does not cover damage caused by improper care, environmental conditions, or unauthorized modifications. For full details, please contact our service department.

Service & Support

Our clients enjoy complimentary piano tuning within the first year of purchase, and free delivery within 1000 miles of our showroom. Beyond the warranty, our team is available to support you with maintenance, restoration, or resale guidance.

Our concierge team is here to assist you with warranty or service inquiries.
