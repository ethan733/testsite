Meet the Founder

Founded by Dan Wilson in 1995 — trusted by musicians worldwide.

Hello!

My name is Dan Wilson, and I founded World Class Piano Gallery in 1995. We started small, selling only entry level console pianos across the Southeastern USA. We quickly grew, and began selling grands as well.

In 2008, I began a new venture, selling used Steinway grand pianos wholesale, to over 40 dealers here in the USA, as well as 12 other countries. To date, we have bought and sold over 1,400 Steinway grand pianos, both wholesale and retail. We are one of the top dealers (for both wholesale AND retail) for used Steinway grand pianos in the world.

We also offer an incredible selection of pre-owned Yamaha, Kawai, Baldwin, Mason & Hamlin, Samick, Young Chang, and MANY MANY other brands.

  • PAY ON DELIVERY: You don't pay until it's delivered. If you don't like it, we will load it back up, and you owe us NOTHING!
  • Free delivery within 1,000 miles from Atlanta (31 States!).
  • At least a 3-year common sense warranty.
  • Free first tuning.

No gimmicks. No bait-and-switch. Just an honest, fair deal on a good piano.

If you're tired of the nightmare of information overload from pushy, aggressive salesmen trying to sell you what THEY want you to have, give us a call. You'll notice a difference immediately.

If we don't have what you're looking for, we can probably get it. If you just need some good piano advice, we are here to help!
